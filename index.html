We want to keep doing the things we enjoy while continuing to reduce our impact. We are conscious of our impact on the planet, and our goal is to do more with less — because that is the definition of sustainability.

Sector 01

Renewable
energy

Henderson Racing is climate conscious, and all fabrication is powered with solar energy. We invested in solar several years ago and haven’t looked back.

Our solar panel software shows us exactly how much CO₂ we have prevented from contributing to air pollution — not an estimate, a readout.

SOURCE 100% solar-powered fabrication

Sector 02

Recycling

Oils, solvents, cleaners and other hazardous materials are part of any motorsports team. Our team respects where these come from — and where they can be responsibly returned. Petroleum products, batteries and tires used by our team are responsibly managed and returned or recycled.

On a hot day at the track, there is no doubt there will be single-use plastic bottles and soda and beer cans. We provide recycling receptacles at our pit so anyone can recycle them.

RETURNED Petroleum products · batteries · tires

Sector 03

Philanthropy

When we are local, we buy local. We support the hotels, restaurants and small businesses of the amazing cities and towns that host the events that make our racing habit possible.

POLICY Every race weekend, spent locally
