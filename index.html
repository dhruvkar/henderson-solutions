Service 01

Assess your chemical footprint

An accurate chemical inventory is key to not only hazard communication, but also for operating a future fit business. We build yours, then keep it honest:

  • Manage materials per RCRA and OSHA regulations
  • Maintain a current chemical inventory with Safety Data Sheets
  • Track chemicals with mobile technology
  • Implement best practices and standard operating procedures using a six-step formula for success

Service 02

Go beyond compliance

Compliance is the floor, not the finish. Using the data from your inventory and hazard communication program, we evaluate the entire program against your business goals to reduce costs.

Then we go further: purchasing and disposing of chemicals using sustainable practices, so your operation is ready for what regulators — and customers — ask next.

We practice what we preach

Henderson Racing is driving to neutral.

Yes — we run a race team, and yes, it still burns fuel. That question makes some teams cringe. Not us. Car #21 is built by solar: the panels on our roof power every tool in its fabrication, and our solar software shows exactly how much CO₂ we’ve kept out of the air. Our goal is to do more with less — that is the definition of sustainability.

S1 · Renewable energy

All fabrication is powered with solar energy. We invested years ago and haven’t looked back.

S2 · Recycling

Petroleum products, batteries and tires are responsibly returned or recycled — and our pit has recycling receptacles for everyone’s bottles and cans.

S3 · Buy local

When we race, we buy local — supporting the hotels, restaurants and small businesses of the towns that host us.
